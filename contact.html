Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance, especially during busy seasons like holidays. However, we often have same-day availability for urgent needs - just give us a call!

Please bring your pet's vaccination records (for first visits), any special shampoos or medications, and a list of any behavioral concerns or special instructions. We'll take care of everything else!

A full groom typically takes 2-3 hours, while basic services like nail trims take 15-30 minutes. We'll provide an estimated time when you book your appointment based on your pet's specific needs.

Currently, we operate as a salon-based service where you drop off and pick up your pet. However, we're exploring mobile grooming options for the future. Contact us to stay updated on new services!
