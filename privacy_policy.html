1. Overview

Welcome to Snazzy Paws Grooming. We are committed to protecting your privacy and the confidential information about your beloved pets. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us.

Key Points:
  • We only collect information necessary to provide excellent grooming services
  • Your pet's health and behavioral information is kept strictly confidential
  • We never sell or rent your personal information to third parties
  • You have control over your data and can request updates or deletion at any time

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when booking appointments or using our services:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Emergency Contacts: Alternative contact person and their phone number
  • Payment Information: Billing address and payment method details (processed securely through our payment processor)
Pet Information

To provide the best care for your pet, we collect:

  • Basic Information: Pet's name, breed, age, weight, and size
  • Health Information: Vaccination records, allergies, medical conditions, and medications
  • Behavioral Information: Temperament, fears, preferences, and special handling requirements
  • Grooming History: Previous services, preferred cuts, and styling notes
  • Photos: Before and after grooming photos for our records and with your permission, marketing materials
Website Usage Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, and navigation patterns
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device (see Cookies section below)

3. How We Use Information

Service Delivery
  • Schedule and manage appointments
  • Provide personalized grooming services
  • Maintain grooming and health records
  • Process payments and billing
  • Send appointment reminders and confirmations
Communication
  • Respond to inquiries and customer service requests
  • Send important updates about your pet's care
  • Provide grooming tips and pet care advice
  • Emergency contact in case of incidents
Marketing Communications (Optional)

With your explicit consent, we may send you:

  • Special offers and promotions
  • Seasonal grooming tips and reminders
  • New service announcements
  • Pet care newsletters

You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails or contacting us directly.

4. Information Sharing

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

Veterinary Emergencies

If your pet requires emergency veterinary care during grooming, we may share relevant health information with veterinary professionals.

Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or regulatory authority (such as animal control or health departments).

Service Providers

Trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business (payment processors, appointment software, etc.).

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards
  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure, encrypted database storage
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Firewall and intrusion detection systems
  • Regular security audits and assessments
Administrative Safeguards
  • Staff training on privacy and security protocols
  • Limited access to personal information (need-to-know basis)
  • Background checks for all employees
  • Signed confidentiality agreements
  • Regular privacy compliance reviews
Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Immediately secure the affected systems
  • Assess the scope and nature of the breach
  • Notify affected customers within 72 hours
  • Cooperate with law enforcement and regulatory authorities
  • Provide free credit monitoring services if financial data is involved

6. Your Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

Access Rights

Request a copy of all personal information we have about you and your pet. We will provide this within 30 days of your request.

Correction Rights

Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information in your records.

Deletion Rights

Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and business requirements for record retention.

Opt-Out Rights

Opt out of marketing communications or request restrictions on how we process your information.
